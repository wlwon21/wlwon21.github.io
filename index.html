The Future of Streaming Services

The success of streaming services is no secret, certainly not with them being so ingrained into our lives. Due to the vastly different licenses streaming services have, one needs to subscribe to multiple streaming services. Yet this is becoming increasingly difficult as these services increase their prices. These prices beg the question, what does the future hold for the streaming landscape?.
